Voxel-geodesic skinning — the good stuff

This computes weights by flooding a solid voxel volume of your mesh and measuring geodesic distance from every bone. It's what keeps joints from bleeding through to the other limb — but it's real number-crunching:
  • Runs in a background thread so the tab stays alive, but expect several seconds to a minute depending on resolution and mesh size.
  • Your fans may spin up. A progress bar will show each phase.
  • If it's too slow, drop the volume resolution or use Fast preview instead.

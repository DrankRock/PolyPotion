◉Camera use (MoCap)

The MoCap and Motion tools can use your device’s camera for pose, hand, and face tracking. The camera feed is processed entirely on your device — it is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to the Service Provider or any third party. Camera access is only requested when you start a live capture and stops when you leave the tool.

What PolyPotion needs to become the tool everyone reaches for

You've already built something rare: a 14-tool character pipeline that runs entirely in the browser, brews nothing to a server, and holds together under one cozy theme. This report is the honest gap between that and the default tool a hobbyist or indie dev opens without thinking. Findings are tagged by priority; the five that matter most are pulled out at the end.

⚗ THE ONE-LINE VERDICT

The tools are further along than the product. The craft inside each workspace is impressive; what's thin is everything around the craft — getting in (onboarding), getting out (export), not losing work (safety), staying alive offline (distribution), and letting others extend it (the module contract). Fix those five rings and the feature set you already have becomes a tool people evangelize.

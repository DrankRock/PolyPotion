The playground audit — reach everyone, then let them go wild

Chapters I and II made the suite honest (offline, safe, exportable) and fluent (the formats the 3D world speaks). Four brews later the machine is sound — this sweep found no rot in the engines. What it found instead: the door is narrower than the room. The suite is mouse-only, its offline promise has holes at the edges, its library UI predates a shelf of hundreds of characters, and — for an app meant to be fun for anyone — there is still no tool whose only job is joy. Chapter III is those four stories.
